Privacy

AgeofScreen Privacy Policy

AgeofScreen is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not run a cloud service that stores your screenshots, recordings, or edited media by default.

What AgeofScreen Stores

  • Your captures, exports, and project files are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share them.
  • Basic app preferences may be stored locally so the app can remember settings such as onboarding or editor preferences.

What We Do Not Collect By Default

  • We do not upload your screenshots or recordings to our servers by default.
  • We do not require an account to use the core local recording and editing features.

Third-Party Services

If you download AgeofScreen through a third-party store or platform, that platform may process purchase, licensing, analytics, or crash data according to its own privacy policy.
